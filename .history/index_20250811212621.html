ABOUT US

De-Brilliant House

De-Briliant House, founded by Mrs. Adeyinka Oluwafunmilayo, is an academy committed to unlocking the brilliance in every learner through technology. We provide a welcoming space where children, teens, and adults can gain hands-on skills in coding, robotics, design, and other digital fields. Our goal is to not only teach tech but also inspire problem-solving, creativity and confidence equipping learners to thrive in a fast-changing digital world.
